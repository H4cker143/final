How it Works (The Alice & Bob Demo)

  • Alice uploads her original art here. We compute a unique cryptographic hash directly in her browser (the file never leaves her device).
  • We bind that hash to Alice's identity on the Polygon Smart Contract permanently recording the exact timestamp.
  • Bob downloads Alice's art and tries to claim it as his own on this platform.
  • The smart contract rejects Bob instantly. It mathematically proves the hash already belongs to Alice, protecting her intellectual property forever.
